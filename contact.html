About our bakery

Sweet Legacy is a dessert space built on compassion in Oradea, Romania. We bake fresh treats that delight families while helping children in need access education, opportunity, and support.

Reach out for custom dessert orders, mission partnerships, event collaborations, or any way you would like to create impact with us.

Send us a message

  • How does my order help children?

    A portion of every Sweet Legacy purchase supports child-focused initiatives, including education resources, safe development programs, and opportunities for long-term growth.

  • Do you offer custom dessert orders?

    Yes. We create custom cakes, celebration dessert boxes, and event menus. Share your occasion and we will design something warm, beautiful, and unforgettable.

  • Can organizations partner with Sweet Legacy?

    Absolutely. We welcome schools, nonprofits, and community groups to partner with us for fundraising, awareness campaigns, and local support initiatives.

  • How can I stay updated on your impact?

    Follow our social channels and newsletter for impact updates, seasonal dessert launches, and stories showing how your orders help create real change.
